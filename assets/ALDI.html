SintLUCAS

Brand Identity

Sufficient exercise is essential for a healthy life. If we look at the number of people who meet the exercise guidelines, there is still a lot to gain. As a practice exam Sammi had to create a campaign for Kenniscentrum Sport en Bewegen.

Problem

In the Netherlands, almost 1 in 6 children between the ages of 4 and 18 are overweight. Childhood obesity is common. The cause of obesity is often too much unhealthy or fatty food and too little exercise. Children are not outside anymore, distracted by their phone.

Idea

As we know our phones are one of the biggest distractions. The question is how can we use our phone to exercise? Sammi thought of the idea of exercising using your phone and came up with the idea to create a mobile app where users can gain points using the stairway and eventually win prizes.

Result

For the visuals Sammi decided to use DALL-E. He wanted to experiment with visuals made by AI. How can we make an abstract artwork of using the stairway with AI. A result of thousands of exports came out of this using text prompts. After selecting the best generated images he made it complete with text and extra visual aspects.
